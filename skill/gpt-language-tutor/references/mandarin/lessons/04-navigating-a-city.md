# Lesson 04 — Navigating a City

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| ……在哪里？ | ... zài nǎli? | where is...? | productive |
| 怎么走？ | zěnme zǒu? | how do I get there? | productive |
| 请再说一遍 | qǐng zài shuō yí biàn | please say that again | productive |
| 远吗？ | yuǎn ma? | is it far? | productive |
| 地铁站 | dìtiězhàn | subway station | exposure |
| 洗手间 | xǐshǒujiān | restroom | exposure |
| 左边 | zuǒbian | left side | exposure |
| 右边 | yòubian | right side | exposure |
| 前面 | qiánmian | ahead; in front | exposure |
| 很近 | hěn jìn | very near | exposure |

## Main task

Find the subway station, confirm whether it is far, and repeat the key direction back.

## Changed retry

Ask for the restroom instead, and use the repair phrase after one deliberately faster direction.

## Correction priorities

- Keep `左 zuǒ` and `走 zǒu` distinct in the direction exchange.
- Use `在 nǎli` as part of the full location question.

## Carry forward

Reuse `请` from lesson 03 as part of the clarification request.
