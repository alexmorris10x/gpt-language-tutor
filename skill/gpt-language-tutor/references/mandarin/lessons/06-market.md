# Lesson 06 — Shopping at a Market

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 这个多少钱？ | zhège duōshao qián? | how much is this? | productive |
| 我要两个 | wǒ yào liǎng ge | I want two | productive |
| 太贵了 | tài guì le | too expensive | productive |
| 可以便宜一点吗？ | kěyǐ piányi yìdiǎn ma? | can it be a little cheaper? | productive |
| 这个 | zhège | this one | exposure |
| 那个 | nàge | that one | exposure |
| 苹果 | píngguǒ | apple | exposure |
| 香蕉 | xiāngjiāo | banana | exposure |
| 一斤 | yì jīn | half a kilogram; one jin | exposure |
| 好的 | hǎo de | okay | exposure |

## Main task

Ask the price of fruit, choose a quantity, react to the price, and complete the purchase politely.

## Changed retry

Buy a different fruit by weight instead of by item.

## Correction priorities

- Use `两 liǎng` before the measure word `个`.
- Keep `便宜 piányi` as a connected word with a light final syllable.

## Carry forward

Reuse `多少钱？` from lesson 05 and `我要……` from earlier transaction lessons.
