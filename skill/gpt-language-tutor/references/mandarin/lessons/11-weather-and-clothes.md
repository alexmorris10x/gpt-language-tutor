# Lesson 11 — Weather and Clothes

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 今天天气怎么样？ | jīntiān tiānqì zěnmeyàng? | how is the weather today? | productive |
| 今天很…… | jīntiān hěn... | today is very... | productive |
| 我需要…… | wǒ xūyào... | I need... | productive |
| 你穿什么？ | nǐ chuān shénme? | what are you wearing? | productive |
| 热 | rè | hot | exposure |
| 冷 | lěng | cold | exposure |
| 下雨 | xiàyǔ | rain; raining | exposure |
| 有风 | yǒu fēng | windy | exposure |
| 外套 | wàitào | jacket | exposure |
| 雨伞 | yǔsǎn | umbrella | exposure |

## Main task

Check the weather, describe it, and choose what to wear or carry.

## Changed retry

The forecast changes from hot to rain; revise the plan and choose one different item.

## Correction priorities

- Distinguish `穿 chuān` from `船 chuán` with the correct initial and tone.
- Use `有风` as a complete weather description.

## Carry forward

Reuse a park or city destination while deciding what is needed outside.
