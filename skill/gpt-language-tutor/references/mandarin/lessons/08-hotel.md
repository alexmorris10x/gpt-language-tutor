# Lesson 08 — Checking into a Hotel

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 我有预订 | wǒ yǒu yùdìng | I have a reservation | productive |
| 我叫…… | wǒ jiào... | my name is... | productive |
| 无线网密码是什么？ | wúxiànwǎng mìmǎ shì shénme? | what is the Wi-Fi password? | productive |
| 早饭几点？ | zǎofàn jǐ diǎn? | what time is breakfast? | productive |
| 酒店 | jiǔdiàn | hotel | exposure |
| 护照 | hùzhào | passport | exposure |
| 房间 | fángjiān | room | exposure |
| 房卡 | fángkǎ | room key card | exposure |
| 早饭 | zǎofàn | breakfast | exposure |
| 请帮我 | qǐng bāng wǒ | please help me | exposure |

## Main task

Check in under a name, receive the room key, and ask about Wi-Fi and breakfast.

## Changed retry

The reservation is not found immediately; repeat the name and use `请帮我` to continue.

## Correction priorities

- Keep `预订 yùdìng` distinct from the surrounding words.
- Place the time question after the topic in `早饭几点？`.

## Carry forward

Retrieve the name exchange from lesson 01 in a new transactional context.
