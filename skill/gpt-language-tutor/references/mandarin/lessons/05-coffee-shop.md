# Lesson 05 — At a Coffee Shop

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 我要…… | wǒ yào... | I would like... | productive |
| 热的还是冰的？ | rè de háishi bīng de? | hot or iced? | productive |
| 大杯还是小杯？ | dà bēi háishi xiǎo bēi? | large or small? | productive |
| 带走 | dàizǒu | takeaway | productive |
| 咖啡 | kāfēi | coffee | exposure |
| 茶 | chá | tea | exposure |
| 热的 | rè de | hot | exposure |
| 冰的 | bīng de | iced | exposure |

## Main task

Order one customized drink and answer the barista's two choice questions.

## Changed retry

The chosen size is unavailable; revise only that part of the order.

## Correction priorities

- Hear and answer the `还是` choice without repeating the entire question.
- Keep the two syllables of `咖啡 kāfēi` clear.
