# Lesson 07 — Taking a Taxi

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 师傅，去…… | shīfu, qù... | driver, go to... | productive |
| 去这个地址 | qù zhège dìzhǐ | go to this address | productive |
| 要多长时间？ | yào duō cháng shíjiān? | how long will it take? | productive |
| 在这里停 | zài zhèli tíng | stop here | productive |
| 酒店 | jiǔdiàn | hotel | exposure |
| 机场 | jīchǎng | airport | exposure |
| 地址 | dìzhǐ | address | exposure |
| 左转 | zuǒ zhuǎn | turn left | exposure |
| 右转 | yòu zhuǎn | turn right | exposure |
| 到了 | dào le | we have arrived | exposure |

## Main task

Show a destination, ask the travel time, follow one route question, and request the final stop.

## Changed retry

Traffic blocks the usual route; accept a different route and change the stopping point.

## Correction priorities

- Keep `地址 dìzhǐ` distinct and intelligible as one word.
- Use `在这里停` as a direct but neutral instruction.

## Carry forward

Reuse left and right from lesson 04 in the route choice.
