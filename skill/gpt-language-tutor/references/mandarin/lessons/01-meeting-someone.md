# Lesson 01 — Meeting Someone

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 你好 | nǐ hǎo | hello | productive |
| 我叫…… | wǒ jiào... | my name is... | productive |
| 你叫什么名字？ | nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? | what is your name? | productive |
| 很高兴认识你 | hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ | nice to meet you | productive |
| 我 | wǒ | I; me | exposure |
| 你 | nǐ | you | exposure |
| 名字 | míngzi | name | exposure |
| 也 | yě | also | exposure |
| 再见 | zàijiàn | goodbye | exposure |
| 你呢？ | nǐ ne? | and you? | exposure |

## Main task

Meet a new classmate, greet them, say your name, ask their name, and respond politely.

## Changed retry

Meet a second person and lead the exchange instead of answering first.

## Correction priorities

- Keep `名字 míngzi` connected, with a light neutral `zi`.
- Preserve the basic question rhythm in `你叫什么名字？`.

## Carry forward

Reuse `我叫……` and `你呢？` naturally in lesson 02.
