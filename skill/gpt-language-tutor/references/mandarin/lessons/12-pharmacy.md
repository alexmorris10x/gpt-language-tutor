# Lesson 12 — At a Pharmacy

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 我不舒服 | wǒ bù shūfu | I don't feel well | productive |
| 我……疼 | wǒ... téng | my ... hurts | productive |
| 已经……天了 | yǐjīng... tiān le | it has been ... days | productive |
| 这个药怎么吃？ | zhège yào zěnme chī? | how do I take this medicine? | productive |
| 头 | tóu | head | exposure |
| 肚子 | dùzi | stomach; belly | exposure |
| 发烧 | fāshāo | have a fever | exposure |
| 咳嗽 | késou | cough | exposure |
| 药 | yào | medicine | exposure |
| 一天几次？ | yì tiān jǐ cì? | how many times a day? | exposure |

## Main task

Describe one simple symptom and duration, then ask how the labeled medicine should be taken.

## Changed retry

The pharmacist asks about a second symptom; clarify that you do not have it and repeat the main concern.

## Correction priorities

- Put the body part between `我` and `疼`.
- Keep `药 yào` distinct from `要 yào` through context and phrase rhythm.

## Carry forward

Use `请再说一遍` from lesson 04 if the medicine instructions are unclear.
