# Lesson 10 — Making Plans

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 你想一起……吗？ | nǐ xiǎng yìqǐ... ma? | do you want to ... together? | productive |
| 什么时候？ | shénme shíhou? | when? | productive |
| 好啊 | hǎo a | sure | productive |
| 对不起，我没时间 | duìbuqǐ, wǒ méi shíjiān | sorry, I don't have time | productive |
| 今天 | jīntiān | today | exposure |
| 明天 | míngtiān | tomorrow | exposure |
| 周末 | zhōumò | weekend | exposure |
| 一起 | yìqǐ | together | exposure |
| 吃饭 | chīfàn | eat a meal | exposure |
| 看电影 | kàn diànyǐng | watch a movie | exposure |

## Main task

Invite a friend to one activity, negotiate the day or time, and confirm the plan.

## Changed retry

The friend is unavailable at the first time; offer another time or decline politely.

## Correction priorities

- Keep the activity inside the `你想一起……吗？` question frame.
- Use `没时间` for not having time.

## Carry forward

Reuse `我想……` from lesson 02 to suggest a personal preference.
