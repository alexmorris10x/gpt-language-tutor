# Lesson 02 — A Day at the Park

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 我想…… | wǒ xiǎng... | I want to... | productive |
| 你想做什么？ | nǐ xiǎng zuò shénme? | what do you want to do? | productive |
| 我喜欢…… | wǒ xǐhuan... | I like... | productive |
| 你喜欢……吗？ | nǐ xǐhuan... ma? | do you like...? | productive |
| 公园 | gōngyuán | park | exposure |
| 走一走 | zǒu yì zǒu | take a walk | exposure |
| 跑步 | pǎobù | run; jog | exposure |
| 坐一下 | zuò yíxià | sit for a bit | exposure |
| 拍照 | pāizhào | take photos | exposure |
| 喝水 | hē shuǐ | drink water | exposure |

## Main task

Decide with a friend what to do in the park and express one real preference.

## Changed retry

The weather becomes hot; choose a different activity and explain the choice using the same core patterns.

## Correction priorities

- Keep `想 xiǎng` distinct from `像 xiàng` by using the correct third tone.
- Place the activity directly after `我想` or `我喜欢`.

## Carry forward

Reuse `你呢？` from lesson 01 once, without drilling it.
