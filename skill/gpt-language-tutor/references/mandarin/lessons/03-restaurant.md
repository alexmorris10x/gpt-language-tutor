# Lesson 03 — Being in a Restaurant

## Language set

| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 我要…… | wǒ yào... | I would like... | productive |
| 请给我…… | qǐng gěi wǒ... | please give me... | productive |
| 不要辣 | bú yào là | not spicy | productive |
| 买单 | mǎidān | the bill, please | productive |
| 菜单 | càidān | menu | exposure |
| 米饭 | mǐfàn | rice | exposure |
| 面条 | miàntiáo | noodles | exposure |
| 水 | shuǐ | water | exposure |
| 茶 | chá | tea | exposure |
| 谢谢 | xièxie | thank you | exposure |

## Main task

Order one dish and one drink, specify that the dish should not be spicy, and ask for the bill.

## Changed retry

The restaurant is out of the first dish; choose a replacement without restarting the conversation.

## Correction priorities

- Use `不要辣` as one complete preference phrase.
- Keep `请 qǐng` short and clear before the request.

## Carry forward

Reuse `我喜欢……` to express a genuine food preference.
