# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, constructive, and considerate. Welcome people regardless of identity, background, experience, language ability, or disability. Focus criticism on ideas and code, accept feedback in good faith, and protect learner privacy.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, personal attacks, trolling, mocking a person's language ability, and publishing private information are not acceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other spaces where someone represents the project.

For a private conduct report, use the repository's [private reporting form](https://github.com/alexmorris10x/gpt-language-tutor/security/advisories/new) and start the title with `Code of Conduct`. Do not put sensitive conduct reports in a public issue. The maintainer will review reports in good faith and may remove content, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
